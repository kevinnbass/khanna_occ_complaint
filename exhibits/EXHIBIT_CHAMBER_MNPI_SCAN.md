## 8. Scope and caveats

- Window definitions are ±30 days around NDAA enactment, NDAA markup, and CMS rulemaking dates. A tighter ±14-day variant exists in the underlying tables; rate numbers drop by roughly 40–60% when windows narrow.
- Sector tagging hit 10,200 of 124,944 PTR rows (8.2% coverage). Mutual funds, bond CUSIPs, limited-partnership interests, and private-company shares remain untagged. Under-tagging tilts the analysis toward overt-ticker Members and against family-LP-heavy Members. McCaul alone has 10,819 untagged trades — his true defense exposure may be materially larger than the $10.33 million visible here.
- Upper-bound dollar sums use the high end of each PTR bracket (for example, $1M–$5M is scored as $5M). Actual dollars are uniformly distributed within the bracket; a midpoint estimate divides aggregate figures by approximately two.
- Boundary dates for each window category are embedded in the underlying scan; treat boundaries as approximate.

## 9. Reproduce

```sql
-- Top by dollar volume
SELECT member_last_name, sample_state_district, n_tx_total, n_tx_sector_tagged,
       dollar_max_sum_windowed, dollar_max_sum_def_prime_ndaa_win,
       dollar_max_sum_pharma_cms_win, pct_sector_windowed
  FROM public.house_ptr_mnpi_scan_by_member
 WHERE n_tx_sector_tagged >= 5
 ORDER BY dollar_max_sum_windowed DESC NULLS LAST
 LIMIT 25;

-- Top by rate
SELECT member_last_name, sample_state_district, n_tx_total, n_tx_sector_tagged,
       pct_sector_windowed, pct_def_prime_ndaa_win, pct_pharma_cms_win,
       dollar_max_sum_windowed
  FROM public.house_ptr_mnpi_scan_by_member
 WHERE n_tx_sector_tagged >= 10
 ORDER BY pct_sector_windowed DESC NULLS LAST
 LIMIT 20;

-- McCaul GE NDAA
SELECT transaction_date, transaction_type, amount_min, amount_max, owner, windows_hit
  FROM public.house_ptr_mnpi_scan_per_tx
 WHERE member_last_name='McCaul' AND canonical_ticker='GE'
   AND (in_ndaa_enact_window OR in_ndaa_markup_window)
 ORDER BY transaction_date;

-- 100% pharma x CMS outliers
SELECT member_last_name, sample_state_district, pct_pharma_cms_win, n_tx_pharma_total
  FROM public.house_ptr_mnpi_scan_by_member
 WHERE pct_pharma_cms_win >= 100.0 AND n_tx_pharma_total >= 5;
```
